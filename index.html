Maybe the Greatest Anime of all time

I also happen to be a huge anime fan, so i decided to write about what i think is the greatest anime of all time. Which happens to be Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood. This anime is a story or two brothers, who lose parts of their body while trying to revive their dead mother, with the use of alchemy. What i really like about this anime is that it has a little bit of everything and all the characters have their own development so you can relate to their mothivations a lot better. While i am not doing this anime justice with this summary, this is the highest rated anime ever.
